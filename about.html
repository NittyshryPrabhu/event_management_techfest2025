About TechFest 2025

TechFest 2025 is a three-day interdisciplinary festival that celebrates technology, creativity, and community. We bring together students, professionals, startups, and educators to explore innovations across software, hardware, design, and entrepreneurship.

When: 20–22 December 2025   •   Where: MIT Meerut Engineering Campus, Uttar Pradesh

Mission & Vision

Mission: To create a hands-on platform where students can learn cutting-edge technology, collaborate on projects, and get exposure to industry experts. We focus on practical skills, teamwork, and exposure to career pathways.

Vision: To become a regional hub that connects young innovators with mentors, startups, and industry partners — fostering a culture of continuous learning and entrepreneurship.

Tracks & Activities

TechFest divides the program into focused tracks so participants can choose experiences that match their interests.

Hackathon

48-hour team challenge: build, pitch, win cash prizes and incubation support.

AI & ML

Hands-on workshops, live demos, and mentorship in machine learning workflows.

Web & Mobile

Frontend, backend, and mobile development workshops and mini-project showcases.

Design & UX

Design sprints, prototyping sessions, and portfolio reviews by professionals.

Robotics & IoT

Hardware workshops, robot demos, and IoT competitions for makers.

Startup Alley

Pitching sessions and mentor clinics for early-stage startups and student founders.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend?

All students from colleges and universities are welcome — both individual participants and teams.

Is there an entry fee?

Most workshops are free; certain paid workshops or competitions will have a small fee listed on the registration page.

How do I register?

Use the registration form on the main landing page or the "Register" button in the nav. Teams should register together with one representative.

Are accommodation and travel provided?

We provide a list of nearby hotels and limited on-campus accommodation on request. Travel support is not currently available.

Venue & Logistics

TechFest 2025 will be held on the MIT Meerut Engineering Campus. The venue includes auditoriums for keynotes, labs for workshops, and an open ground for exhibitions and cultural events.

How to reach

Nearest railway station: Meerut Junction (7 km). Nearest airport: Meerut (approx. 220 km). Local buses and autos are available from the station.

Day-wise Venue Map

Main auditorium (block B), Workshops (labs block F), Startup Alley (ground east), Food & Cultural (ground west).

Code of Conduct

TechFest 2025 is committed to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. Participants are expected to be respectful. Harassment or abusive behaviour will not be tolerated and may result in removal from the event.

If you witness or experience any misconduct, please contact the event team immediately (contact details below). All reports will be handled confidentially.
